About Passive Steve

Passive Steve is an interactive-entertainment channel on YouTube. Hosted by me, Steve (Stephen Falepau). There is two videos every week. One video on Tuesdays 3pm PST/Wednesdays 12pm NZT and the second video on Thursdays 3pm PST/Fridays 12pm NZT.

The channel was launched in 2015 and is a part of the Smosh Games Alliance.
